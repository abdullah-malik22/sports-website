Popular Types of Sports

Sports can be categorized into many different types, depending on various factors like location, equipment, and participants. Here are some of the most popular types:

Team Sports

These are sports played by teams of players. Examples include soccer, basketball, and rugby.

Individual Sports

These are sports where individuals compete on their own. Examples include tennis, athletics, and swimming.

Combat Sports

These sports involve direct physical contact between opponents. Examples include boxing, martial arts, and wrestling.

Water Sports

Sports performed in or on water. Examples include rowing, swimming, and surfing.

Adventure Sports

Sports that require a high level of physical exertion and often take place in natural, rugged environments. Examples include rock climbing, skiing, and mountain biking.
